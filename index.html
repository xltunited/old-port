My Passion

My enthusiasm for building apps comes from a unique mixture. Since I was a kid, I've loved interacting with computers. Aside from simply playing on them, I wondered how computers "thought". That and an affinity to learn natural languages, combined into an interest in Computer Science. There is no other field where I feel my skills would be utilized to their maximum other than programming. It is a joy to solve problems through programming, to create, to make something unique, and then to innovate.
